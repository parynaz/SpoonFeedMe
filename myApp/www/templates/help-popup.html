This app can read the instructions of the recipe out loud to you!

You can begin using this feature by clicking 'Activate Voice'

Then you can control everything with your voice

Try saying the following:

Read/Repeat

Read the current step

Next

Read and Display next step

Back/Previous

Read and Display the last step

Finish/Quit/Stop

Stop voice activation

How much/How many

Read the quantity of any ingredients in current step

How much/How many {Ingredient}

Read the quantity of specified ingredient in current step
